Snag Decay Class Dynamics Basal Area Grid

This grid is created by the Snag Decay Class Dynamics behavior. It holds the amount of basal area for live and cut trees. Maps and grid resolution changes for this grid are not honored.

Data in the grid

Data member nameDescription
Live BA Per HaThe amount of basal area, in square meters per hectare, of live adults.
Cut BA Per HaThe amount of basal area, in square meters per hectare, of trees harvested this timestep.
