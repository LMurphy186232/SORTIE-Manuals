Substrate favorability Grid

This grid is created by either the Substrate Dependent Seed Survival No Gap Status or the Substrate Dependent Seed Survival With Gap Status behavior. It holds the proportion of seeds expected to germinate on the substrate composite of the grid cell. The cell resolution must match the "Substrate" grid, above.

Data in the grid

Data member nameDescription
Favorability Index - Species XThe proportion of seeds of Species X expected to germinate in that cell
